Program Overview

Our production engineering internship program offers a unique opportunity to work with cutting-edge rice milling technology while developing practical skills in manufacturing and process optimization.

What You'll Learn

  • Rice milling machine operation and maintenance
  • Production process optimization
  • Quality control and testing procedures
  • Manufacturing workflow management

Program Structure

  • 1 Week 1-2: Introduction to rice milling technology
  • 2 Week 3-4: Hands-on machine operation training
  • 3 Week 5-6: Process optimization projects
  • 4 Week 7-8: Quality control and final project

Benefits

  • Competitive stipend package
  • One-on-one mentoring
  • Certificate of completion
  • Potential full-time opportunity

Who Should Apply?

Essential Requirements:

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Mechanical Engineering, Production Engineering, or related field
  • Basic understanding of manufacturing processes and machinery
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
