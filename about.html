About Sufi Engineering

At the time of its founding in 1982, Sufi Engineering primarily focused on producing machinery for the oil industry, such as oil expelling machines. However, due to a decline in market demand, the company was forced to shift its focus and in the late 1990s, it began producing machinery for the flour industry. A few years later, Sufi Engineering also entered the rice industry, further expanding its product offerings.

Today, Sufi Engineering is known for producing high-quality, reliable machinery for the rice industry. Our product line includes a variety of machines such as rice polishers, paddy separators, and husk aspirators. All of our products are designed and built to the highest standards of quality and durability, ensuring that they will provide reliable performance for many years to come.

In addition to our standard product line, we also offer custom-designed machinery to meet the specific needs of our customers. Our team of experienced engineers and technicians work closely with customers to understand their unique requirements and develop customized solutions to meet their needs.

At Sufi Engineering, we are committed to providing the best possible service to our customers. We offer technical support and maintenance services to ensure that your machinery is always running at its best. We have a network of service centers around the world to provide local support for our customers, ensuring that they have access to the resources they need to keep their machinery in top condition.

Thank you for considering Sufi Engineering for your rice processing needs. We look forward to the opportunity to work with you and provide you with the best machinery and service in the industry.

Sufi Engineering is a leading manufacturer and provider of cutting-edge rice milling machinery. With a commitment to innovation and quality, we strive to empower rice mills worldwide with our advanced rice polishers and processing solutions.

At Sufi Engineering, we take pride in our extensive experience and expertise in the rice industry. Our team of skilled engineers and technicians work diligently to develop state-of-the-art rice polishing machines that optimize efficiency and deliver consistent rice quality.

We value the success of our customers above all else. Our goal is to enable rice mills to flourish by providing them with reliable and sustainable solutions that lead to increased production capacity, reduced costs, and a competitive edge in the market.

Sufi Engineering is dedicated to environmental responsibility. Our rice polishers are designed with energy-saving features, contributing to a greener and more sustainable rice milling industry.

Whether you are a small-scale mill or a large rice processing facility, Sufi Engineering is here to support you with personalized solutions and exceptional customer service. Join us on the journey towards enhanced productivity and success in the rice industry.
