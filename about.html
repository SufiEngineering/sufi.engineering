Dedicated to Shaping the Future of Rice Milling Worldwide

At Sufi Engineering, our mission is clear: to empower rice mills worldwide with cutting-edge solutions that elevate productivity, reduce costs, and foster sustainability. We are committed to delivering not only state-of-the-art machinery but also unparalleled service, ensuring our clients thrive in the competitive rice industry.

Established in 1982, Sufi Engineering began its journey as a manufacturer of machinery for the oil industry, specializing in oil expelling machines. As market dynamics evolved, we adapted, shifting our focus to the flour industry in the late 1990s. Later, in the pursuit of diversification, we entered the rice industry, expanding our product range and establishing ourselves as a prominent player in the field.

Tailored Services for Flourishing Rice Mills.

Sufi Engineering takes pride in offering a comprehensive range of high-quality machinery tailored to the rice industry's diverse needs. From precision rice polishers to efficient paddy separators and husk aspirators, our machines are crafted with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring robust performance and longevity. But our commitment doesn't end with manufacturing – we provide personalized solutions, technical support, and maintenance services, ensuring our clients' machinery operates at peak efficiency.
