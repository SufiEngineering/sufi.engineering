Who We Are

Engineering Excellence
Since 1982

Sufi Engineering was founded in Okara, Punjab with a singular vision — to bring world-class engineering to Pakistan's rice industry. What began as a small workshop has evolved into the country's most trusted name in rice mill machinery.

Today, we design, manufacture, and install everything from individual polishing machines to complete turn-key rice processing plants. Our machines process millions of tonnes of rice every year, serving customers from Punjab to the Middle East, Africa, and Central Asia.

Every machine we build carries our founding promise: precision engineering that delivers results.
