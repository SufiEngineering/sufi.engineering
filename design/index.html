Frequently Asked Questions

Is 2D drawing really free?

Yes, we provide a basic 2D layout drawing for free to help you visualize your concept. This includes major dimensions and a PDF view. Detailed engineering drawings and source files are part of our paid 3D services.

Do you provide CAD files?

Upon upgrading to our 3D CAD service, we provide standard industry formats like DXF, STEP, STL, or IPT (SolidWorks compatible). These are ready for any CNC machine worldwide.

Why is 3D design important?

3D modeling allows us to simulate movement, check for part clearances, and perform stress calculations. It virtually eliminates manufacturing errors that are often missed in 2D views.

Can I use the design for manufacturing elsewhere?

Absolutely. While we offer internal laser cutting and fabrication, our engineered designs are production-ready for any manufacturer. We focus on being engineering experts first.

How long does design take?

Free 2D drawings are typically delivered within 24–48 hours. Complex 3D machine designs or complete system layouts take between 3 to 10 working days depending on scope.
