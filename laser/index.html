Capabilities

What We Cut

Our CNC laser cutting machine handles a wide range of materials and thicknesses with industrial precision.

Mild Steel

Up to 20mm thickness. Ideal for structural components, brackets, and frames.

Stainless Steel

Up to 12mm thickness. For food-grade, medical, and corrosion-resistant parts.

Aluminum

Up to 8mm thickness. Lightweight parts for automotive and industrial use.

Galvanized Steel

Up to 6mm thickness. Weather-resistant parts for outdoor and HVAC applications.

Copper & Brass

Up to 5mm thickness. Decorative, electrical, and artistic applications.

Custom Alloys

Specialized cutting for spring steel, tool steel, and other industrial alloys.
